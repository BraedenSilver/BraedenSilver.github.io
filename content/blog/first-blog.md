## Welcome to My Blog!

Hey, I’m Braeden. I’m into macro and black-and-white photography, and I’m all about blending art, tech, and business. This blog will cover everything I’m passionate about, from photography techniques and gear reviews to engineering projects, business tips, and practical tutorials. I’m currently a business student with a lot of other interests.

## What to Expect

Here’s what I’ll be sharing:
- **Photography**: Especially macro and black-and-white shots. Macro is my favorite type of photography because it lets me see things on a micro-scale that our eyes usually miss.
- **Gear Reviews**: Honest takes on the gear I use, whether it’s lenses or accessories. My reviews will be practical and informative.
- **Business Tips**: Learn with me as I build my online photography business, mixing creativity with entrepreneurship.
- **Engineering Projects**: Though I’m focused on business, I still like to tinker with engineering, which was my original major in college.
- **Tutorials**: Step-by-step guides on how I do what I do, whether it’s building a Hugo website, setting up GitHub Pages, or photography techniques. I’ll also show how I save time and money along the way.

### Possible Ideas
- **Writing** I enjoy writing, so I might write and share short stories just for fun!
- **Personal Updates** I’ll also share general life updates, like things going on at school or other personal stuff.
- **Other Interests** I love working with Source Engine, so I might post about that too. I’m also into programming and thinking about learning a new language (maybe German). We’ll see where it goes!
