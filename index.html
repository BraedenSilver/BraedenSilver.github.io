About

I am an Electrical and Computer Engineering student at Texas State University who loves building useful, approachable hardware. My work spans firmware, PCB design, and the interfaces that tie everything together.

A few highlights live in the project portfolio, including autonomous sensing rigs and playful outreach builds. I jot down the messy lab notes on the blog, share longer-term research on the research page, and post schematics and code on GitHub.

I enjoy collaborating with curious people, so feel free to reach out or connect on LinkedIn.
